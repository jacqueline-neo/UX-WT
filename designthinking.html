Empathise

Creating a persona that will fit the target audience description

The target audience is young adults who have not travelled before or in awhile and wants to know more travelling information like best place, time and activities to plan for their trip.
A persona fitted for the target audience

Lisa Tan

Software Engineer

Purpose Statment: Lisa wants to go overseas with her friends
Age: 25
Interests: Enjoys hiking

    Lisa take to decide on a country to travel:
  • Find out the list of countries that is worth going to based on her interests and the basic information provided.
  • Find more necessary information about the unique selling point going to the country interested after seeing the list.
  • Choose the type of travel that significantly stands out after seeing all the countries.
  • Look at other people experience on the shortlisted choices.
  • Take measures to find additional enquiries that is not listed in the website.

Define

Identifying Problem Statements

Problem
Users want to be able to find the list of countries and the necessary information to decide where to go on their trip.
Users want to hear genuine feedback to know whether the trip is worth to go.
Users want to ask more questions that is not stated in the website but are important to know before booking plane tickets, accomodation and activities.

Ideate

Solutions to the Problem Statements

Solutions
The list of countries is at the homepage and easily accessible on the navigation bar. Basic information is included in the flip card and the read more button. The dropdown button shows external useful links user can also see.
The portfolio page that is also on the navbar shows the different categories of the type of travel and the price range as these two are the main factors before a user decides.
The enquiry page and the sign up event helps user to further decide on the country based on personal interactions with other people experience.
